Our Story

Briana & Chris met in 2010 while studying together at UMKC and living in the same dorm building. With mutual friends and classes, they spent a lot of time getting to know each other both in and out of class.

As time went on, Briana approached Chris and they started dating in the Spring of 2011.

After many summers and a whole year of Chris's masters studies spent apart, Briana and Chris became engaged when Chris popped the question in a corn field shortly after Briana relocated to Illinois to be with Chris.

Mutual passions include music, animals, laughing, and dessert.
